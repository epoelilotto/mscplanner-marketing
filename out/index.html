How it works

A guided flow that takes a student from blank canvas to a valid, coordinator-ready plan in under fifteen minutes.

  1. 1

    Pick track + profile

    Mandatory courses pre-load automatically based on the student's specialisation.

  2. 2

    AI suggests electives

    Students describe their interests; the planner recommends electives with explanations grounded in the catalogue.

  3. 3

    Real-time validation

    EC totals, PPD credits, quarter workload, and prerequisite conflicts are checked as the plan is built.

  4. 4

    Share with advisor

    One-click share link. Advisors review and comment without creating an account.

Common questions

How long does it take to set up for my programme?
About two weeks. You send us your programme guide; we encode the validation rules and you review them before going live. No IT involvement needed on your side.
What does it cost?
Pricing is per-institution. We'll give you a number in the first call — no commitment needed to hear it.
Is student data safe and GDPR-compliant?
Yes. Plans are stored locally in the student's browser by default — no data reaches our servers unless the student explicitly shares their plan. All shared data is stored on EU infrastructure and never used for training or sold to third parties.
Does it connect to our student information system?
Not yet. StudyPlanner works standalone today — students use it alongside whatever SIS you have. Integration with SIS and course catalogues is on the roadmap.
What if our programme rules change each year?
We update the validation logic each academic year as part of the subscription. Send us the new programme guide and we'll handle the rest.
Can students use it without creating an account?
Yes. Plans are saved in the browser by default. Students can optionally create an account to sync across devices and share with their advisor.
